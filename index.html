Mission of WWF

(World Wildlife Fund)

Our mission is to conserve nature and reduce the most pressing threats to the diversity of life on Earth.

We organize our work around these four areas:

Climate

Create a climate-resilient and zero-carbon world.

Forest

Conserve the worlds most important forests to sustain nature's diversity.

Wild-Life

Ensure the worlds most iconic species are secured and recovering in the wild.

Oceans

A resilient ocean sustains marine life and functioning ecosystems that support rich biodiversity.

About Us

The worlds leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by more than one million members in the United States and close to five million globally.Unique working way of WWF combines global reach with a foundation in science, involves action at every level from local to global, and ensures the delivery of innovative solutions that meet the needs of both people and nature.
